Holographic reconstruction
Digital holography and 3D morphometry of physical and biological samples.

Optical & photonic sensing
Fibre optic and underwater optical sensing for environmental monitoring.
Applied computer vision
ML-assisted imaging pipelines and real-time depth processing.
